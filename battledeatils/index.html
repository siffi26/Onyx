REAL STEEL

INTRODUCTION :

Does your robot have ability to rule the world? It’s the time to prove it…

Presenting, an incinerating zone of terror for robots, Real steel– The Robo War.
A scintillating war of Robots. So be ready to fight, conquer, defeat and rule a kingdom full of threats.
Here you will have to eliminate the other team by fighting with a warrior robot.

Robot Size
At starting position, Robot must fit into a box of size 60cm X 60cm X 60cm and maximum permitted weight of bot is 60kgs.
Power Specification
The maximum D.C. voltage supply at any time must not exceed 24V onboard.
An external 220V AC will be provided.

Note : Any team using any different form of energy must make the necessary arrangement on their own. They must confirm it with organizers beforehand for safety reasons.

 

Weapon Specification
Each Robot must possess at least one attacking weapon.
Before the beginning of any round the robots will be tested for safety and maximum size and weight criterion.

Rules

  • A team can consist of a maximum 6 participants. Each participant must have a valid college identity card. The round finishes at the end of 3 minutes or when one team knocks out the opposing team, whichever occurs first.
  • In each case the Robot must start from given starting dimension. The timer would be stopped for rearranging positions.
  • Use of any mechanism that damages the arena would lead to disqualification.
  • Dragging the wired Robot once it is placed on the track may lead to disqualification.
  • Fire, water, chemicals are not to be used as weapons.
  • Lego kits/parts are not allowed.
  • There may be simple amendments to the rules which will be announced before starting the game.
  • The decisions of judges will be final and binding.
  • Judges and organizers reserve the right to modify the rules. Participants are required to check for the updates.

 

FORMAT OF TOURNAMENT:

 

There will be a ROUND ROBIN type tournament. Every team will battle every other team in the tournament. In the end, two teams with maximum points will be selected to play final. The final battle will decide the winner.

 
Game

The event will be played in three rounds of 3 minutes each.
In each round, both teams battle their bots in the arena. The team who wins the max rounds is declared as the winner

POINTS:
10 points for winning a round

5 point for a draw

0 point for losing a round

1 point for a significant hit with weapon.

NOTE: Only significant hit will be considered for a point. It is decided by the judges .
A team wins if  : 
It knocks out the opponent out of the arena.
Else if the other team is made non operational for 20 seconds
Else if it has more points than other team at the end of round after 3 minutes.
It fulfills the given round qualifying conditions :
Else the team with best design wins which will be decided by judges on the basis of innovative weapon, better strategy, good mobility and efficient controls.
